Contributors

Panopticon AI is searching for undergraduate contributors to help develop our platform. Developers will work with our CTO, Minh Hua. Please view our existing code here. We're looking for contributors who have experience in or are eager to learn the following and can contribute at least 5 hours a week:

  • Frontend: Developing and maintaining a visualization web app for simulations using OpenLayers, React, TS
  • Backend: Developing a simulation engine and web server using Python (C++ experience desired)
  • Data Science: Developing AI/RL agents that can play warfare scenarios

    • While the project is open to any contributor under the Apache 2.0 License, we currently only have the capacity to mentor one student to train them on DevOps in a real software engineering environment. As a two person team, we are not paid - and unfortunately, this mentorship program is also unpaid. However, we believe that our mentorship will provide valuable engineering skills and help build your portfolio in your early career.

    To apply as a mentee, send an email to luke@panopticon-ai.com with the subject "Student Contributor" a resume, and a paragraph about your motivation for applying to this project.
