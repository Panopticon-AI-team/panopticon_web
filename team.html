Our Story

Drawing from years of firsthand experience and a clear vision for revolutionizing military training, we founded Panopticon AI out of a pressing need to address the inadequacies we observed within the Department of Defense's existing simulation tools.

Frustrated by the clunky, outdated, and user-unfriendly nature of current offerings, we set out to create a solution that is not only accessible to the warfighter on the ground but also sophisticated enough for researchers conducting advanced reinforcement learning.

Our platform delivers a modern, web-based simulation environment that bridges the gap between operational readiness and cutting-edge technological research, ensuring that our defense forces are equipped with a tool that is as intuitive as it is powerful.
