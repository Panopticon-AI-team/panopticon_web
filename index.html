Reinforcement Learning

Panopticon AI is still under development. Inspired by the technologies behind AlphaStar and OpenAI, our system will utilize state-of-the-art reinforcement learning algorithms for wargaming simulations. Rather than simply analyzing data to provide analysis and summarization, our software will decide and act intelligently in a simulated environment. We hope to close the OODA loop like no other technology has done before.
