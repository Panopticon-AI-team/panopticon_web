Panopticon AI is a web-based software simulation platform for miltiary training and operations, with source code available for researchers. We're bringing the power of simulation to every member of the Department of Defense.


Applications

Realistic Exercise Simulations

Simulate the most realistic red and blue forces available at the unclassified level. Easily integrate our reinforcement learning agents into your custom platform, so that you can train in realistic scenarios any time using a fraction of the manpower.

Training

Our platform provides tutorials and training on strategic and operations concepts for the joint force. Train on broad concepts to learn about air missions, strategy, and joint operations, without having to spend time on expensive in-person courses

Wargaming

Simulate strategic scenarios with a flexible and customizable web platform. No need for dice or game boards - you can create your own scenarios without specialized training.

Getting started

We are developing as a source-available platform for modeling and simulation researchers and are actively seeking contributors to our project. To get started, read the docs. Our platform is under construction - we are building scenario creation to set up unique basing scenarios across the world, a reinforcement learning environment, and customizable unit data.

Complete the OODA loop

Reinforcement Learning

Panopticon AI is inspired by the technologies behind AlphaStar and OpenAI. Our system provides a platform for researchers to train and run reinforcement learning algorithms for wargaming simulations. By training agents, military planners will be able to realistically forecast military scenarios and challenge existing assumptions about theater-level strategy. We're hoping to foster the creation of a military AI that no human can defeat.
