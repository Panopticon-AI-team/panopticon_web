The web app for military simulation, data display, and AI training

Panopticon AI lets you create and play custom military scenarios and visualize military AI agent behavior in an open and common data format. Released under the Apache 2.0, the platform is free for commercial use.


Applications

Compatibility with AI training

Our platform BLADE is built to be compatible with multiple types of AI, including reinforcement learning (Gymnasium) and LLMs.

Interoperable data format

Our scenario data format is open and easy to translate to other data formats in military modeling and simulation. Record a real-time scenario and share it it with other users.

Visualize model behavior

LLMs are language models and can’t easily be visualized. BLADE lets you put agentic models into a map and see how agents act in military scenarios for free.

Getting started

Our source code is available here. Information for interested code contributors is also on our github. To understand the features we have available, click Play or read the docs. Or, join our invite-only Discord.

Reinforcement Learning

Panopticon AI is inspired by the technologies behind AlphaStar, the AI program which powered the computer players in the real-time-strategy game Starcraft. Our system provides a platform for researchers to train and run reinforcement learning algorithms for wargaming simulations. By training agents, military researchers will be able to explore more powerful models and strategies for training AI in an ecosystem of researchers. We're hoping to foster the creation a network of people who can catalyze additional advancements in military AI.
